Let me introduce myself.

Hi, I'm Long. I am a student at FPT University - majoring in Software Engineering. The reason I studied this major was because since I learned about computers, I have had a strong passion for it. I spent a lot of time learning about computers. After that, I started working with photo and video editing software. Thanks to that, I got a part-time job as a photographer, cameraman and video editor. But that was not enough, so I decided to major in Software Engineering so I could learn more about computers and software.
